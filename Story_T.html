Tadpole is very happy!

La-la-la, la la, la-la-la, thanks for letting me sing with you! In return, I want to tell you a story...

I used to be made fun of for sounding different from the other Tadpoles.
I was ostracised but still wanted to be accepted so I hung around the other tadpoles. One day I noticed these snakes slithering around the area and alerted the other tadpoles with my unique voice. That day I gained many friends and learned to accept my unique voice.

I love singing so let me sing you the next clue to find Eel:
A lazy eel is fast asleep by the building that connects,
By the doors there are some stairs, go down it’s not complex,
A little red house it hides itself in,
But what is not hidden is its tail fin.
